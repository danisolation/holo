# Milestone v4.0: Paper Trading & Signal Verification

**Status:** ✅ SHIPPED 2025-07-20
**Phases:** 22-26
**Total Plans:** 10

## Overview

Kiểm chứng chất lượng tư vấn AI bằng giả lập trading — mọi AI trading signal tự động thành lệnh ảo có thể đo lường. Full analytics dashboard với equity curve, drawdown, streak tracking, calendar heatmap, và so sánh hiệu suất theo direction/confidence/sector/timeframe.

## Phases

### Phase 22: Paper Trade Foundation

**Goal**: Paper trade data model with correct state machine and P&L calculation exists and is verified by unit tests
**Depends on**: Phase 21 (v3.0 trading signals must exist to track)
**Requirements**: PT-02, PT-03, PT-05, PT-07
**Plans**: 2 plans

Plans:
- [x] 22-01: Data layer — PaperTrade + SimulationConfig models, Alembic migration 013, model tests
- [x] 22-02: Business logic — state machine, P&L calculation, position sizing service + TDD tests

**Details:**
- PaperTrade model with 7 TradeStatus states, 2 TradeDirection, 22+ columns
- State machine: PENDING → ACTIVE → PARTIAL_TP → CLOSED_{TP2,SL,TIMEOUT,MANUAL}
- SimulationConfig singleton (100M VND default, auto_track=true, min_confidence=5)
- Position sizing with 100-share lot rounding
- 44 TDD unit tests for business logic

### Phase 23: Position Monitoring & Auto-Track

**Goal**: System automatically creates paper trades from valid signals and monitors positions daily for TP/SL/timeout hits
**Depends on**: Phase 22
**Requirements**: PT-01, PT-04, PT-06, PT-08
**Plans**: 2 plans

Plans:
- [x] 23-01: Auto-track signals job — creates PENDING paper trades from valid AI signals with dedup
- [x] 23-02: Position monitor job — daily SL/TP/timeout evaluation, PENDING activation, BEARISH support

**Details:**
- `paper_trade_auto_track` job chained after `daily_trading_signal_triggered`
- `paper_position_monitor` job chained after `daily_price_crawl_upcom`
- SL-first on ambiguous bars (conservative approach)
- D+1 open entry to prevent lookahead bias
- Timeout: swing 15 trading days, position 60 trading days
- Score=0 signals excluded from tracking

## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**
- SL-first on ambiguous bars — conservative approach when both SL and TP breach same day
- D+1 open entry — prevents lookahead bias
- Streaks counted per-trade (not per-day)
- Confidence brackets: LOW (1-3), MEDIUM (4-6), HIGH (7-10)
- react-activity-calendar renderBlock for dual-color (green win / red loss) heatmap
- BEARISH framing tracks "avoided loss" since VN retail cannot short sell

**Issues Resolved:**
- PT-09 manual follow: full backend existed but no frontend form — fixed with Follow button on DirectionColumn
- AN-09 sector analysis: hook existed but no component rendered it — fixed with SectorCard table

**Issues Deferred:**

- ADV-02: Multiple virtual accounts for A/B testing strategies
